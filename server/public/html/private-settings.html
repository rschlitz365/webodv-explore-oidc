Private Settings

Private views and settings are stored in your browser's data, not on our server. Normally, such private content is lost when you delete your browser data.

To avoid loss of your private settings press the Save Settings button before deleting the browser's data. After you deleted your browser data, select the saved settings file under Restore Settings to restore your private settings. Check the Delete existing settings before restoring to delete the existing private settings before restoring from the file.
