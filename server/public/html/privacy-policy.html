Privacy Policy

This webODV Explore site requires login via your D4Science account or by using your institutional or social media credentials. You are using this site as a named user.

What information do we collect?

We collect anonymized usage statistics using the Matomo software running at the Alfred Wegener Institute. In addition, when you are exiting a data exploration session we log a usage record containing the connection IP address and the session duration. Private views and settings are stored in your browser's data, not on our server. Note that such private content is lost when you clear your browser data.

What do we use your information for?

The sole purpose of the logged usage records is to monitor usage of this webODV Explore site. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties the logged usage records.

Your consent

By using our site, you consent to our website privacy policy.

Changes to our privacy policy

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page. This policy was last modified on 2024-04-01.
